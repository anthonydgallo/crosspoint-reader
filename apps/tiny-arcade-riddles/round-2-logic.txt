TINY ARCADE: ROUND 2 (LOGIC)

Puzzle A: Three Switches
You are outside a room with 3 light switches.
Inside the room is 1 bulb. You may enter the room only once.
How do you determine which switch controls the bulb?

Puzzle B: Coin Flip Race
You and a friend each flip one fair coin.
If both coins match, you win.
If they differ, your friend wins.
Should you go first, second, or does order not matter?

Puzzle C: Door Guards
Two doors: one leads to safety, one to trouble.
Two guards: one always tells truth, one always lies.
You may ask one guard one yes/no question.
What do you ask to pick the safe door?

Puzzle D: River Crossing Lite
A fox, a chicken, and grain must cross a river in a boat
that can carry only you plus one item.
If left alone: fox eats chicken, chicken eats grain.
How do you get all three across safely?
