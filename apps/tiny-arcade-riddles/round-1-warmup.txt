TINY ARCADE: ROUND 1 (WARMUP)

Try to solve before turning to the Answers entry.

1) I speak without a mouth and hear without ears.
I have no body, but I come alive with wind.
What am I?

2) The more you take, the more you leave behind.
What are they?

3) What has many keys but cannot open a single lock?

4) I have cities, but no houses; forests, but no trees;
and water, but no fish. What am I?

5) What can travel around the world while staying in one corner?

Bonus lightning round:
- What gets wetter the more it dries?
- What has one eye but cannot see?
- What begins with T, ends with T, and has T in it?
