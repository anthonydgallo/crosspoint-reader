TINY ARCADE ANSWERS

Round 1:
1) An echo.
2) Footsteps.
3) A piano.
4) A map.

Bonus:
- A towel.

Round 2:
Puzzle A:
Turn switch 1 on for a while, then off.
Turn switch 2 on and enter room.
If bulb is on -> switch 2.
If bulb is off but warm -> switch 1.
If bulb is off and cold -> switch 3.

Puzzle B:
Order does not matter; each player has a 50% chance.

Puzzle C:
Ask either guard:
"If I asked the other guard whether this door is safe,
would they say yes?"
Then choose the opposite of that answer.

Puzzle D:
Take chicken across. Return alone.
Take fox across. Bring chicken back.
Take grain across. Return alone.
Take chicken across.
