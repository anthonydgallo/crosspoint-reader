STRENGTH SNACK (8 MIN)

Set a timer: 40 sec work, 20 sec rest.
Repeat each move twice.

1) Squats
2) Incline push-ups (desk or wall)
3) Reverse lunges (alternate legs)
4) Plank hold (desk or floor)

Scale options:
- Easier: reduce depth, slower pace
- Harder: add a pause at hardest point

Finish:
1 minute easy walk + deep breathing.
