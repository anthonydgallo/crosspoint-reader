DESK MOBILITY (6 MIN)

No equipment. Gentle range of motion.

Minute 1
Neck: slow half-circles + shoulder rolls.

Minute 2
Thoracic twist: arms crossed, rotate left/right.

Minute 3
Hip opener: seated figure-4 stretch, switch sides.

Minute 4
Ankles and calves: heel raises + ankle circles.

Minute 5
Wrist reset: palm stretch, reverse stretch, shake out.

Minute 6
Breathing cooldown: inhale 4, exhale 6.

Pain rule:
Stretch tension is okay. Sharp pain is a stop sign.
