MOSCOW MULE

Spicy, refreshing, and easy to make.
The ginger beer does the heavy lifting.

INGREDIENTS
- 2 oz vodka
- 1/2 oz fresh lime juice
- 4-6 oz ginger beer
- Lime wheel for garnish

METHOD
1. Fill a copper mug (or highball glass)
   with ice.
2. Add vodka and lime juice.
3. Top with ginger beer.
4. Stir gently to combine.
5. Garnish with a lime wheel.

GLASS: Copper mug (traditional) or
highball glass

TIP: Use ginger beer, not ginger ale.
Ginger beer has a much stronger, spicier
flavor that defines this cocktail.
