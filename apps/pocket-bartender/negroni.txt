NEGRONI

Bitter, bold, and beautifully balanced.
An Italian aperitivo icon.

INGREDIENTS
- 1 oz gin
- 1 oz Campari
- 1 oz sweet vermouth
- Orange peel for garnish

METHOD
1. Add gin, Campari, and sweet vermouth
   to a mixing glass with ice.
2. Stir for about 30 seconds until
   well chilled.
3. Strain into a rocks glass over a
   large ice cube.
4. Express an orange peel over the
   drink and drop it in.

GLASS: Rocks glass

TIP: Equal parts make this recipe easy
to remember and scale. For a lighter
version, top with soda water to make
an Americano.
