MOJITO

Light, minty, and perfect for summer.
A Cuban classic.

INGREDIENTS
- 2 oz white rum
- 1 oz fresh lime juice
- 2 tsp sugar (or 3/4 oz simple syrup)
- 6-8 fresh mint leaves
- Soda water
- Lime wheel and mint sprig for garnish

METHOD
1. Gently muddle mint leaves with sugar
   and lime juice in a tall glass. Don't
   shred the mint -- just press to release
   the oils.
2. Add rum and stir to combine.
3. Fill the glass with ice.
4. Top with soda water.
5. Stir gently to mix.
6. Garnish with a lime wheel and a
   fresh sprig of mint.

GLASS: Highball / Collins glass

TIP: Slap the mint sprig between your
palms before garnishing to release its
aroma.
