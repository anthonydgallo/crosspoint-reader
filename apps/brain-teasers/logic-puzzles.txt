LOGIC PUZZLES

Think carefully before checking the answer.

---

1. A man is looking at a photograph.
Someone asks, "Whose picture are you
looking at?" He replies: "Brothers and
sisters I have none, but that man's
father is my father's son."
Who is in the photograph?

Answer: His son.

---

2. You are in a room with three light
switches. Each controls one of three
bulbs in the next room. You can only
enter the next room once. How do you
figure out which switch controls which
bulb?

Answer: Turn switch 1 on for 10 minutes,
then turn it off. Turn switch 2 on. Enter
the room. The warm bulb is switch 1. The
lit bulb is switch 2. The cold, dark bulb
is switch 3.

---

3. A farmer needs to cross a river with a
fox, a chicken, and a bag of grain. His
boat can only carry one item at a time.
The fox will eat the chicken if left
alone, and the chicken will eat the grain.
How does he get everything across?

Answer: Take the chicken across first.
Go back, take the fox across. Bring the
chicken back. Take the grain across.
Go back and take the chicken across last.

---

4. You have 8 identical-looking balls.
One is slightly heavier. Using a balance
scale only twice, how do you find the
heavy ball?

Answer: Weigh 3 vs 3. If balanced, weigh
the remaining 2 against each other. If
unbalanced, take the heavier group of 3
and weigh 1 vs 1. If balanced, the third
is heaviest. If not, the scale tells you.

---

5. Two fathers and two sons go fishing.
They each catch one fish, but only three
fish are caught total. How?

Answer: They are a grandfather, father,
and son. The father is both a father and
a son.
