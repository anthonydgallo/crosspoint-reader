WORD PUZZLES

Play with letters and language.

---

1. What 8-letter word can have a letter
taken away and it still makes a word?
Take another letter away and it still
makes a word. Keep going until you have
1 letter left.

Answer: Starting -- starting, staring,
string, sting, sing, sin, in, I.

---

2. I am a word of 5 letters. Take away
the first letter and I am the name of
a country. Take away the first two
letters and I am the opposite of the
5-letter word. What am I?

Answer: Woman. Oman. Man.

---

3. What word in the English language does
the following: the first two letters
signify a male, the first three letters
signify a female, the first four letters
signify a great male, while the entire
word signifies a great female?

Answer: Heroine.

---

4. What common English word has three
consecutive double letters?

Answer: Bookkeeper.

---

5. Forward I am heavy, backward I am
not. What am I?

Answer: The word "ton" (backwards: "not").

---

6. What disappears as soon as you say
its name?

Answer: Silence.

---

7. I have 4 letters. I become shorter
when you add 2 letters to me. What am I?

Answer: The word "short" -- add "er" to
get "shorter."

---

8. What 5-letter word becomes shorter
when you add two letters to it?

Answer: Short.
