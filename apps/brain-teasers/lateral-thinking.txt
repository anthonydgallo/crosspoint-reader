LATERAL THINKING

These puzzles require creative thinking.
The answer is often not what you expect.

---

1. A man pushes his car to a hotel and
tells the owner he's bankrupt. Why?

Answer: He's playing Monopoly.

---

2. A woman shoots her husband, holds him
under water for five minutes, then hangs
him. Thirty minutes later, they go out
for dinner together. How?

Answer: She took a photograph of him.
She shot the photo, developed it in
water, and hung it to dry.

---

3. A man walks into a bar and asks the
bartender for a glass of water. The
bartender pulls out a gun and points it
at him. The man says "thank you" and
walks out. Why?

Answer: The man had the hiccups. The
bartender scared them away.

---

4. There are 20 people in an empty room.
A man enters and shoots 19 of them. Yet
he walks out and nobody stops him. Why?

Answer: He is a photographer. He shot
their pictures.

---

5. A man is found dead in a field. Next
to him is an unopened package. There are
no other people or creatures around.
How did he die?

Answer: The package was a parachute
that failed to open.

---

6. A man lives on the 20th floor of an
apartment. Every morning he takes the
elevator down to the ground floor to go
to work. When he returns, he takes the
elevator to the 10th floor and walks up
the remaining 10 flights. Why?

Answer: He is too short to reach the
button for the 20th floor. He can only
reach as high as the 10th floor button.
